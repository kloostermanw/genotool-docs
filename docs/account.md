# Account Management

This page covers everything related to your GenoTool user account, from registration to profile management.

## Creating an Account

To start using GenoTool:

1. Go to [app.genotool.com](https://app.genotool.com) and click **Register**.
2. Fill in your name, email address, and password (minimum 8 characters).
3. Confirm your password.
4. Complete the registration.

After registration, a verification email is sent to your email address. Click the link in the email to verify your account.

!!! note
    If you received a team invitation, you can register using the invitation link. Your account will automatically be added to the team.

## Login / Logout

- Go to [app.genotool.com](https://app.genotool.com) and enter your email and password to log in.
- To log out, click on your name in the top-right corner and select **Logout**.

## Profile Management

To view and edit your profile:

1. Click on your name in the top-right corner.
2. Select **Profile**.

From here you can update:

- **Name** - Your display name (3-50 characters).
- **Email** - Your email address. If you change your email, you will need to verify the new address.
- **Language** - Choose between English and Dutch.
- **Timezone** - Set your preferred timezone.

## Changing Your Password

1. Go to your profile page.
2. Enter your current password.
3. Enter and confirm your new password (minimum 8 characters).
4. Save the changes.

## Deleting Your Account

If you wish to delete your account:

1. Go to your profile page.
2. Click **Delete Account**.
3. You may be asked to provide feedback about why you are leaving.
4. Confirm the deletion.

!!! warning
    Deleting your account removes your account and all teams you own. This action cannot be undone. Active subscriptions must be cancelled before you can delete your account.
